About Smartgreen House

Enabling year-round local food production, even in unpredictable climates.

Smartgreen House was founded to close the gap between traditional hobby greenhouses and complex commercial installations. Many households, cafés and small farms want reliable, high-quality crops but lack time, technical expertise and stable weather conditions.

Our systems combine automated climate control, irrigation and lighting with a simple user interface. Sensors constantly monitor temperature, humidity, soil moisture and light levels, adjusting conditions to match plant needs. The result is consistent growth, fewer failures and higher yields with minimal manual work.

Every Smartgreen House is engineered around sustainability. We prioritize insulated structures, solar-ready power, closed-loop water systems and durable materials that perform well in urban and suburban environments. Customers benefit from lower operating costs and a clearer sustainability story in their own homes or businesses.

  • ✓ Automation that fits non-technical users
  • ✓ Designs optimized for tight urban spaces
  • ✓ Energy-efficient, solar-ready infrastructure
  • ✓ Data-driven insights for better planning
Business fundamentals focus: Smartgreen House targets a clear, growing niche of residential and small commercial customers who need predictable, sustainable production rather than experimental gardening. Our value proposition combines cost efficiency, technology-enabled reliability and strong environmental credentials.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Smartgreen House.

Below are answers to the questions we most often hear from prospective residential and commercial customers. If you need details specific to your location or business model, we are happy to discuss them directly.

  • How much maintenance does a Smartgreen House require?

    Routine maintenance mainly involves checking nutrient and water levels, cleaning filters and observing plants. Most climate, irrigation and ventilation tasks are automated. We provide a simple maintenance checklist and optional service packages for customers who prefer support.

  • Do I need technical skills to operate the system?

    No advanced technical skills are required. The user interface is designed around clear icons and plain language. Pre-set growing programs cover common crops, and our team handles the initial configuration. Training and quick-reference guides are always included.

  • Can the greenhouse work in extreme climates?

    Yes, systems are specified according to local climate data. For hotter or colder regions we add insulation, shading and ventilation capacity, and design suitable control strategies. For very extreme climates, we provide an upfront feasibility assessment during the consultation process.

  • Do you provide installation and after-sales support?

    Yes. Where possible, our team or certified partners deliver and install the greenhouse on site. We support customers remotely during the first growing cycle, and optional maintenance and optimization plans are available for commercial users.

  • What happens if there is a power or internet outage?

    Core climate functions are designed to fail-safe. Short outages generally do not impact crops, while longer outages can be mitigated with backup power and ventilation strategies. We can advise on backup options during project planning, especially for commercial customers.

  • Can the system integrate with existing infrastructure?

    In many cases, yes. We can connect to existing water supplies, solar installations and monitoring systems provided they meet basic technical requirements. This is assessed during the site survey and reflected in the project proposal.
